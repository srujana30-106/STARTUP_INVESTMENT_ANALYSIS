Most startups received small funding amounts, typically less than $1 million.

Venture capital is the most common form of funding across startups.

Funding is concentrated in specific countries or cities, especially in developed markets like the US.

Startups in developed countries generally receive higher average funding amounts.

Markets like Games, FinTech, and HealthTech have high startup counts and attract significant funding.

On average, startups take approximately 4 years to secure their first round of funding after being founded.
